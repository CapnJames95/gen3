IV / EV CALCULATOR
Gen 3 stat formula · IV range finder · EV planner · Pokérus · Nature multipliers
Gen 3 EV system: Unlike later games, Gen 3 has no per-stat cap — a Pokémon can have up to 65,535 EVs in a single stat. However, only the first 255 EVs contribute to each stat (floor(EV/4) is applied), and only the first 252 make a practical difference. The total across all stats is capped at 510. This calculator uses the standard 252 competitive limit per stat, but explains why.
Pokérus (RSE): A rare virus that doubles EV gain from all battles. A Pokémon with Pokérus earns 2× EVs per KO, stacking with EV items. In Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald, the only EV-boosting items are Vitamins (Protein, Iron, etc.) which add 10 EVs each and cap at 100 EVs per stat. Pokérus doubles battle EVs only, not Vitamin EVs.

Gen 3 EV cap quirk: In Gen 3, EVs are stored as a single byte per stat (0–255). The stat formula divides EVs by 4 (flooring), so 252 and 253 give the same result. Having 252 EVs in one stat and 252 in another uses 504 of the 510 total, leaving 6 for a third stat — which gives +1 to that stat. This is the origin of the "252/252/6" spread competitive players use in Gen 3.
A neutral nature (same stat +/−) has no effect. All others give ×1.1 to one stat and ×0.9 to another.
